Your Brief

What we're searching for

A personal bolt hole — rural retreat, roughly 4 hours from Sydney, under $2M. Hilly country with water, seclusion, and an existing house. Not a working farm. Low maintenance, somewhere to disappear to.

The Jugiong property set the benchmark: river frontage, hilltop position, end of road, modest house. That's what everything is scored against.

✓ Under $2M
✓ 30–200 acres
✓ Within 4hrs of Sydney
✓ Existing house
✓ Rural zoning
✓ Target corridors only

Scoring ranks by: water (25pts), terrain (20pts), seclusion (15pts), house quality (15pts), drive time (10pts), park proximity (5pts), carbon eligibility (5pts), and convenience (5pts). Your feedback adjusts these weights over time.

What You'll Receive

Weekly to your inbox

  1. 1 A short email each week with property count, top 3 previews, and a button to open the full shortlist.
  2. 2 An interactive shortlist with every property that meets criteria — photos, scores, map, drive times, and a value badge showing whether it's good value, fair, or premium for its corridor (based on $/acre vs. historical sales). Designed for your phone.
  3. 3 One-tap feedback on each property: Love it, Interesting, or Not for me. Your reactions sharpen the scoring.
  4. 4 Favourites and notes — star properties you want to revisit, add comments. Everything saves to a private sheet that stays between us.

Each property card includes:

✓ Photo
✓ Price
✓ Match score (out of 100)
✓ Value badge (Good Value / Fair / Premium)
✓ Price per acre vs. corridor median

✓ Feature tags (river, dam, views, etc.)
✓ Score breakdown (8 factors)
✓ Map pin with location
✓ Link to original listing
✓ Feedback buttons & notes

No logins, no apps to install. The email is the whole experience — everything you need is right there. The pages linked below are there if you want to dig deeper, but you never have to.
